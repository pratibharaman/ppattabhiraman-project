ABOUT


ART helps artists create.
This website houses a growing archive of books, videos, images, and other sites to guide internet explorers away from getting stuck in algorithmic scrolling and toward a more curiousity-led journey. The goal is to inspire ongoing creativity and grow the overall community of people who make things.

The work is compiled by Pratibha Pattabhiraman, an Oakland resident and fan of artists like Sheila Heti and . Pages will be updated on a monthly basis and will focus on new topics as they arise in local, national, and global politics.
