ABOUT

ACT provides resources, guides, and tools to help community members get involved in politics, meeting them at whatever level they're currently at. Materials provided will align most closely with leftist schools of thought, particularly surrounding Democratic, Socialist, Marxist, and Communist theory. Materials provided will align most closely with leftist schools of thought, particularly surrounding Democratic, Socialist, Marxist, and Communist theory.

The work is compiled by Pratibha Pattabhiraman, an Oakland resident and supporter of PSL (Party for Socialism and Liberation). Pages will be updated on a monthly basis and will focus on new topics as they arise in local, national, and global politics.
